Welcome to the game MONSTER TAMER! 

This game was created as a project for course Data Structure (120).

The main goal is to fight different monsters as you, a hero whose stats can be either chosen or uploaded through a file, walk through
Linked Land Loo. Luck is also an important factor, as it can determine the outcome of your battles!
The number of levels is limitless, because, what's stopping you from taming monsters for eternity?
